Before you hand over any cash, follow this systematic approach to ensure the **flip a boat boat values** align with your profit goals. This process minimizes risk and identifies "hidden" costs that most beginners miss.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Preliminary Research">

  <Step num="2" title="On-Site Hull Inspection">

  <Step num="3" title="Engine Diagnostics">
    Perform a compression test on all cylinders. In 2026, any variance greater than 10% between cylinders is a red flag. Check the spark plugs for signs of water intrusion or running "lean."

  <Step num="4" title="Trailer and Accessory Audit">
    Inspect the trailer frame for structural rust (not just surface rust). Check the tires' date codes; if they are over 5 years old, they must be replaced, which impacts your **flip a boat boat values** calculation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Final Offer Calculation">
    Take your "Estimated Resale Value," subtract "Repair Costs," and subtract your "Desired Profit." The remaining number is your maximum walk-away offer.
  </Step>
</Steps>
