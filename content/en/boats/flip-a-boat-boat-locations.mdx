<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Step-by-Step Boat Restoration Guide">

Once you have secured a hull from one of the active **flip a boat boat locations**, you must transport it to your personal plot for a complete mechanical overhaul. A boat cannot run or be sold for a premium without a fully functioning engine. 

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Claim the Hull">
    Drive to the Scrap Marine, interact with the green claim boundary, and send the starter boat to your plot database.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Source Engine Parts">
    Visit the Marine Shop. Purchase a water pump, air box, CDI box, carburetor, flywheel, fuel filter, propeller, and two spark plugs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Disassemble the Old Motor">
    Return to your plot computer, select 'Edit Boat', remove the engine cover, detach the rusty propeller, and drop the worn-out lower unit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Assemble and Customize">
    Install the new water pump, mount the lower unit back on, replace the internal electrical parts, apply fresh paint, and select premium materials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Replacing every rusty component is vital. The game calculates the condition rating based on the percentage of new parts installed versus old, worn-out parts.

| Component Name | Status | Replacement Priority | Function |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Propeller | Rusty | High | Power Delivery |
| Water Pump | Damaged | Critical | Engine Cooling |
| Spark Plugs (x2) | Worn | High | Ignition |
| Flywheel | Corroded | Medium | Rotational Energy |
| CDI Box | Faulty | Medium | Electrical Timing |
| Carburetor | Dirty | High | Fuel-Air Mixture |

<Callout type="tip" title="Assembly Shortcut">
You do not need to manually carry parts from the shop to your plot. Once purchased at the Marine Shop, all parts are instantly accessible via your plot's build-mode inventory.
</Callout>

</Section>
