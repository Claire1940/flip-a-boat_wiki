To successfully manage your transport, follow these precise steps to ensure your boat reaches the workshop in pristine condition.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Align the Hitch">
    Reverse your truck slowly toward the trailer tongue. Use small steering adjustments to ensure the hitch is centered. An on-screen prompt will appear once you are in the correct range to attach.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Load the Vessel">
    Position the trailer at the edge of the water or the boat's location. Drive or winch the boat onto the trailer beds. Ensure the hull is centered to prevent the trailer from leaning during transit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Secure the Load">
    Interact with the trailer's securing mechanism. This "locks" the boat to the trailer physics, preventing it from sliding off during sharp turns or sudden stops.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Navigate to the Workshop">
    Drive at a moderate speed. The game features realistic wave and boat physics, meaning the weight of the trailer will affect your truck's braking distance and turning radius.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Physics Warning">
Avoid sharp, high-speed turns while the trailer is loaded. The realistic weight distribution can cause your truck to flip or the trailer to disconnect, potentially damaging the boat and reducing your resale margin.
</Callout>
