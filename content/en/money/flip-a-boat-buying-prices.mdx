<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Step-by-Step Boat Flipping Guide">

Executing a perfect flip requires a structured workflow. From the moment you browse the live market to the final handshake on a sale, following a disciplined process ensures you never lose money on a project.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Claim Starting Capital">
    Before purchasing your very first project, open the in-game settings menu and redeem active codes like `release` to claim an instant $10,000 cash injection. This provides a safe repair reserve.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select a Finishable Project">
    Browse the live market and select a boat that fits your current budget. Ensure that the combination of the buying price and estimated repair costs does not exceed your total cash balance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Haul to the Workshop">
    Attach your trailer to your truck, align it carefully with the purchased vessel, secure the hull using the on-screen prompt, and drive cautiously to your designated repair area.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Mechanical Repairs">
    Interact with the highlighted repair points on the boat. Replace critical propulsion systems, steering components, and dashboard electronics before spending any money on cosmetic adjustments.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Final Sale and Reinvest">
    Perform a pre-sale check to ensure all required components are fully restored. Sell the completed vessel to claim your profit, then reinvest your returns into a larger, more lucrative project.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Hauling Physics">
Vehicles in this simulator utilize realistic physics. When hauling long, heavy trailers, take wider turns and begin braking much earlier than usual to prevent jackknifing or losing your load.
</Callout>
