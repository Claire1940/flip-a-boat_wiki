<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step: Launching and Loading">

Perfecting the use of **flip a boat trailers** at the ramp is a skill that separates novices from pros. The game's physics engine simulates the friction of the bunks and the buoyancy of the hull, making every launch a unique challenge based on the weather and tide conditions.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Approach the Ramp">
    Align your truck and trailer perpendicular to the water. Use the first-person camera mode for better precision when backing down the beach or concrete ramp.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Submerge the Trailer">
    Back into the water until the trailer bunks are approximately 75% submerged. Be careful not to submerge the truck's exhaust, especially during high tide or stormy weather.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Release the Winch">
    Unlock the safety chain and winch strap. If you have an electric winch, this process is significantly faster. Ensure the boat is buoyant before fully releasing.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Launch">
    Slowly reverse the boat off the trailer. In Flip A Boat!, you can see the motor move and react to the water as you gain clearance from the trailer frame.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Clear the Ramp">
    Once the boat is free, pull the empty trailer back up to the parking area to avoid blocking other players and to earn "Clean Launch" XP.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Launch Phase | Common Mistake | Professional Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Alignment** | Jackknifing the trailer | Use small steering inputs |
| **Depth** | Backing in too deep | Stop when water hits the fenders |
| **Loading** | Power loading too fast | Use the winch for final 2 feet |
| **Recovery** | Forgetting the drain plug | Check the "Pre-Launch Checklist" |

<Callout type="warning" title="Warning">
Attempting to launch a heavy boat on a single-axle trailer can cause the trailer frame to "flip" or glitch through the map. Always match your trailer's rating to the boat's dry weight plus fuel.
</Callout>
