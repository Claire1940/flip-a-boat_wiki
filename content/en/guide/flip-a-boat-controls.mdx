**How to Recover a Flipped Boat:**
If the boat capsizes during a test drive, you cannot right it using standard controls. You must use the **Recovery Service** from the interaction menu (Tab). This costs a small fee but teleports your boat back to the workshop. To avoid this, always keep your **Center of Mass** low by placing heavy batteries and fuel tanks in the lowest part of the bilge.

<Callout type="warning" title="Engine Flooding">
If a boat remains flipped for more than 60 seconds, the engine will suffer **Water Damage**. This requires a full teardown in the workshop, which can significantly eat into your resale profit margins.
